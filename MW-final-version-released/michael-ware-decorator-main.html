FAQ

Do you charge for a quotation?

No, we offer free written quotation and always come and see the job at a time that suits you.

Do you hang wallpaper?

Yes. Lining, plain, patterned, woven... etc!

Do you carry out exterior work?

Yes, we are happy to discuss all opportunities for exterior work.

Can you supply the materials/paint?

We are happy to advise on and supply all materials. We understand that every job is different and may require specialist products such as a paint for a high traffic area, non-yellowing gloss or treating a problem area like a stain.

What kind of paint do you supply and recommend?

It depends on the job, but we have expert knowledge and experience with trade products from Johnstones, Dulux and Crown to designers brands such as Farrow & Ball, Little Greene and Mylands. If there is a particular brand or product you would like, we are happy to source it for you.

Which parts of Northamptonshire do you work in?

We work across most of the country. In addition to those already mentioned, some frequent locations include the towns of Oakham, Uppingham, Desborough, Thrapston and Rothwell.

We work in villages across the county including Gretton, East Carton, Caldecott, Upper Benefield, Lower Benefield, Brigstock, Geddington, Deene, Wadenhoe, Bulwick, Dingley, Wilbarston, Stoke Albany, Cottingham and Cranford.

If your location isn't listed, don't worry! Get in touch to see if we can come to you

I’m not sure which colour/finish of paint I want. Can you help?

We are happy to discuss and explain the variety of products available to you. We can also provide colour charts for a variety of brands free of charge. Should you require test pots, these can be made up and delivered to you at trade prices upon acceptance of your quote.

When are you available to carry out the work?

Please feel free to give us a ring or drop us an email and we will get back to you as soon as possible with availability.

I have a question which has not been answered here.

We'd be delighted to answer any questions that you may have via phone or email.
